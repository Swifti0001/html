Hello, I am Aspekt.

I am a discord bot designed, and created to help server owners and administrators sync their bans accross all of their servers at once, along with many other moderation options.

Features

  • Online 24/7

    We strive to keep our bot working for our users. That's why we have an outstanding 99.8% uptime!

  • Ban Syncing

    We offer Synced Bans, a super useful tool for server owners/administrators to be able to ban a member from all their servers at once.

  • 100% Free

    Our bot is all about giving back to the hard working discord owners/administrators. It's free. Forever.

Important Commands

  • >>help - shows a list of all the help categories
  • >>bansync [ID] - bans the user from all the servers you have administration or above permissions.
